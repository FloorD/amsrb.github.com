Ruby Hack Night

August 21, 2012

Springest (Rokin 75)

On Tuesday, August the 21st we're having our first Ruby Hack Night at Springest (Rokin 75). We'll start at 19:00 and our event host Springest will provide us with food and drinks!

Work on your own project, hack on open-source, help out others or lead a BoF session (Birds of a Feather). Let us know what you plan to do when RSVP'ing and we'll try to come up with some sort of organized chaos (it's okay to change your mind on the spot).

There are only 40 spots available, so expect this one to sell out (yes, it's free and there will be a waiting list).
